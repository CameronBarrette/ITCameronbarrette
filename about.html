About Me – My Journey Into IT & Cybersecurity

About Me

My name is Cameron Barrette, and I am an aspiring IT and cybersecurity professional from Charlotte, Michigan. I am currently developing my technical skills through hands-on training at Job Corps while building a strong foundation in networking, system administration, and cybersecurity principles.

I am passionate about understanding how systems work, how they are protected, and how technology can be used to solve real-world problems. Every day, I focus on learning something new that brings me closer to becoming a skilled and reliable IT professional.

Education & Technical Training

I am enrolled in the IT/Cybersecurity trade at Job Corps, where I am gaining structured, practical experience with:

  • Networking fundamentals
  • System configuration and troubleshooting
  • IT security concepts and risk management
  • Hardware and software environments
  • Preparing for industry certifications
  • Professional workplace skills and IT career readiness

This training has helped me understand not just how technology works, but why it works the way it does.

Technical Skills & Areas of Focus

I am actively building knowledge and experience in:

  • Computer networking basics
  • Operating systems and system administration
  • Cybersecurity fundamentals and threat awareness
  • Problem-solving and technical troubleshooting
  • Understanding how systems communicate and are secured
  • Learning how real IT environments operate

I enjoy learning the “behind the scenes” of technology and how different components come together to create secure and efficient systems.

Goals & Aspirations

My long-term goal is to build a career in IT and cybersecurity where I can help protect systems, networks, and data from threats while continuing to grow professionally.

After completing my training, I plan to pursue advanced IT training and eventually enter military service in a technical role. I believe the military will further strengthen my discipline, leadership, and technical skills while allowing me to serve a greater purpose.

I am committed to continuous improvement and lifelong learning in the technology field.

Work Ethic & Personal Values

I take pride in being dependable, disciplined, and hardworking. I have been recognized during my training for my determination, willingness to learn, and ability to stay focused on my goals.

Purchasing my first home at a young age and taking on adult responsibilities early in life taught me financial responsibility, maturity, and long-term planning. These experiences shaped my mindset and taught me the value of accountability and perseverance.

Beyond Technology
